About Bored Walk Creations

Bored Walk Creations LLC is a New Jersey-based company focused on high-performance computing infrastructure. We build and operate reliable GPU servers for AI, creative work, and technical projects.

Our approach is simple: build it like we would use it ourselves. That means quality hardware, transparent operations, and direct communication.

Why We're Doing This

After years of personal projects involving AI, 3D printing, electronics, and home automation, we decided to turn our infrastructure into something others can use too. BWC Pro exists to give makers, researchers, and professionals access to serious compute without the usual headaches.
